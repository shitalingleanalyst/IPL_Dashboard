# IPL_Dashboard

1. Project Title

IPL Analytics Dashboard🏏 

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Indian Premier League (IPL) matches, team performance, player statistics, points table standings, and season highlights. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into team success, player achievements, match outcomes, and tournament trends through dynamic visualizations and KPI-driven reporting.

2. Short Description

The IPL Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI report built to help cricket enthusiasts, analysts, coaches, and sports organizations explore IPL performance data. It enables users to analyze team statistics, player performances, match results, tournament standings, and award winners while supporting data-driven sports analysis and decision-making.

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:<br>

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Primary platform for building interactive reports and dashboards.<br>
• 📂 Power Query – Used for data extraction, cleaning, transformation, and preparation.<br>
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used to create calculated measures, rankings, KPIs, and player statistics.<br>
• 📝 Data Modeling – Relationships established between teams, players, matches, and tournament datasets for seamless analysis.<br>
• 📈 Interactive Visualizations – Bar charts, line charts, donut charts, KPI cards, tables, slicers, and custom navigation buttons used for analysis.<br>
• 📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

4. Data Source

Source: IPL Match & Player Statistics Dataset<br>

The dataset contains tournament-related information including:<br>

IPL teams and franchises<br>
Match schedules and results<br>
Team wins and losses<br>
Player profiles<br>
Batting statistics<br>
Bowling statistics<br>
Strike rates<br>
Orange Cap standings<br>
Purple Cap standings<br>
Tournament points table<br>

The data is structured to support sports analytics, performance evaluation, and season-wise IPL reporting.

5. Features / Highlights<br>
• Business Problem<br>

The Indian Premier League generates enormous amounts of match and player data every season. Fans, analysts, commentators, and franchise management often struggle to quickly answer important questions such as:<br>

Which players scored the most runs?<br>
Which bowlers took the highest wickets?<br>
Which teams are performing consistently?<br>
What is the contribution of each team to the tournament?<br>
How do player strike rates compare with runs scored?<br>
What are the current standings and win-loss records?<br>

Analyzing these metrics manually across hundreds of matches and players can be challenging and time-consuming.<br>

• Goal of the Dashboard<br>

The dashboard aims to:<br>

Provide a centralized view of IPL tournament performance.<br>
Track player achievements and season leaders.<br>
Analyze team performance and match outcomes.<br>
Monitor Orange Cap and Purple Cap standings.<br>
Compare batting and bowling statistics.<br>
Support sports analytics and strategic decision-making.<br>

• Business Impact & Insights

🏏 Player Performance Analysis: Quickly identify the highest-performing batsmen and bowlers.<br>
📈 Team Evaluation: Compare team success through wins, losses, and overall contribution.<br>
🎯 Talent Identification: Discover emerging players based on strike rates, runs, and wickets.<br>
📊 Tournament Monitoring: Track season progress through standings, match results, and player rankings.<br>
🏆 Strategic Decision Support: Assist coaches, analysts, and franchises in performance evaluation and match preparation.<br>
📢 Fan Engagement: Deliver an interactive experience for cricket fans to explore IPL statistics and records.<br>
